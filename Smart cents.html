Welcome to SmartCents

SmartCents is a youth-led nonprofit committed to making financial literacy accessible for every student. We create resources, host workshops, and fundraise for schools in need of educational tools focused on budgeting, saving, and investing.

About Us

Founded by a group of passionate teens, SmartCents began as a simple idea: equip students with real-world money skills. With the support of educators and community partners, we now reach schools across the country with our free learning materials and outreach events.

Our Programs

  • Financial Literacy Workshops: Interactive sessions on budgeting, saving, credit, and investing.
  • SmartCents Starter Kit: A free toolkit with worksheets, guides, and activities for teachers and students.
  • Money Mentors: Peer-to-peer mentoring for financial goal-setting and decision-making.
  • Community Drives: Fundraisers and school supply donations for underfunded schools.

Our Impact

In just our first year, SmartCents has:

  • Reached over 1,200 students in 4 countries
  • Donated more than 150 financial literacy kits
  • Hosted 12 community workshops and webinars
  • Partnered with 8 schools and youth centers
